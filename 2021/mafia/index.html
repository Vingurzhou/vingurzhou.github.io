RULES
We want Mafia Mystery to be fun and exciting for everyone who plays, some actions however, ruin the game for other players and must be stopped. It is with this idea in mind that we created these guidelines.
Multiple Accounts
Users are not allowed to use two accounts in a game. This would give them an unfair advantage against other players.
Game Throwing
Play your role correctly, with your team. Don’t sway the game in the wrong direction on purpose. Simply lying about your role, or “revealing” other roles is not against the rules. However, doing those things with the intent to purposefully lose the game for your team is against the rules. As a Mafia, revealing yourself and fellow Mafia members can result in a permanent ban. Killing your own team members is also considered game throwing.
Cheating
● Don’t reveal your role or any information to another player outside the Mafia Mystery app.
● Don’t join or create games just for the purpose of gaining coins.
● Don’t exploit a bug for your own gain.
Inappropriate Behavior
● Don’t spam the chat.
● Don’t intentionally bypass the filter. It’s there for a reason.
● Don’t type anything containing sexual themes, including emojis.
● Profanity or inappropriate content is not allowed your username or status.
● No harassing and threatening other players. Harassment, threats, bullying, and racism won’t be tolerated and are grounds for permanent ban.
● Don’t be excessively annoying.
Impersonating a Player
It is against our rules to change your avatar and username to appear like another player. This includes falsely claiming you are a developer/admin.
Revealing Unreleased Content
If you discover an unreleased avatar, role, or feature: do not share it with others.
Advertising
Do not advertise any service, brand, or product through the chat or your profile. Sharing Discord links is not allowed.

MAFIA INTRODUCTION
Welcome to Mafia! This is a social game of mystery, wit, decisions, and discernment. Once you begin, you will see a village with up to 16 players. Each player is assigned a role and a house. Those aligned with the Mafia must kill off other players until they outnumber the village. Each player votes during the day to execute Mafia members. The role of each player is initially unknown until more evidence is revealed!

VILLAGE
There is one house for each player in the game. Additionally, the three buildings to the north are the Town Hall, Library, and Courthouse. Town Hall: Discuss with other players during the day through a public chatroom.
Library: View the historical records of the village. This includes known roles, murders, and events.
Courthouse: Vote on important village decisions, such as executions.
VILLAGER
Village Wins if all mafia are dead. Sided with the village.

COP
Village Visits one person each night and receives a report of their alignment (mafia or village). Sided with the village.

DOCTOR
Village Visits one person each night and protects them from dying that night. Sided with the village.

SNIPER
Village Can kill one person per game at night. Sided with the village.

GRANNY
Village | Kills anyone who visits her in the night. Sided with the village.

CREEPER
Village Watches one person each night and knows who visits them. Sided with the village.

BULLETPROOF
Village Has an extra life, unless executed by the village. Sided with the village.

GRAVEDIGGER
Village Can talk to dead members at night. May choose one person to revive, but will die in their place. Sided with the village.

DEPUTY
Village Meets with Cops and can arrest one person each night, keeping them from voting the next day. Sided with the village.

TRAVELER
Village Chooses one person to stay with each night and receives visits in the place of their host. Sided with the village.

MAFIA
Mafia Visits and kills one person each night. Wins if mafia outnumber the village. Sided with the mafia.

GODFATHER
Mafia A mafia that is undetectable by cops. Sided with the mafia.

FRAMER
Mafia Visits one person at night. That person will appear guilty to cops. Sided with the mafia.

KAMIKAZE
Mafia Chooses one person to kill during the day. The chosen person and Kamikaze both blow up and die. Sided with the mafia.

TOASTER
Mafia Chooses one person at night. That person’s role is blocked and they die one night later. Sided with the mafia.

INTERROGATOR
Mafia Interrogates one person each night and finds out their exact role. That person has no memory of the interrogation. Sided with the mafia.

MAID
Mafia Visits one person each night and cleans up the body if they die, leaving their role and the cause of death a mystery. Sided with the mafia.

FOOL
Independent Wins if executed by the village during the day. Sided with no one.
